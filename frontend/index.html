Who We Are

Built on Truth,
Sustained by Mercy

Citadel of Truth and Mercy Assembly is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community of believers committed to authentic worship, sound biblical teaching, and compassionate service.



Founded on the unshakeable foundation of God's Word, we believe every person is loved, valued, and called into purpose. Whether you are taking your first steps in faith or have walked with God for decades, you will find a home here — a place to belong, grow, and be sent out.

Our Core Beliefs
What We Believe

Grounded in the Word of God

Our faith is rooted in the eternal truths of Scripture — guiding every sermon, every service, and every life transformed by His grace.

The Holy Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God — our complete guide for life and godliness.

The Holy Trinity

We believe in one God — eternally existing as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — three persons, one divine essence.

Salvation by Grace

We believe salvation is a free gift through faith in Jesus Christ alone — not by works, but by grace through His atoning sacrifice.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present-day work of the Holy Spirit — empowering believers for service, witness, and Christlike living.

The Church Body

We believe the Church is the Body of Christ — called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and proclaiming the Gospel to all nations.

Meet Our Pastor

"Every soul that walks through our doors carries a story God is not finished writing."

Pastor Emmanuel Adeyemi is the founding senior pastor of Citadel of Truth and Mercy Assembly. With over two decades of ministry, he is known for his passionate, Word-centred preaching, his deep pastoral heart, and his unwavering commitment to truth without compromise.

Alongside his wife, Minister Grace Adeyemi, he leads a church family that believes in radical hospitality, transformative worship, and a love that extends far beyond Sunday mornings.
